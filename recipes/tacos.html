Tacos

Fresh ground beef tacos garnished with lime and cilantro

These tacos are simple and easy. I top mine with cilantro, onions, a squeeze of fresh lime and salsa.

  • prep: 10 minutes
  • cook: 2 minutes
  • total: 12 minutes
  • yield: 8 tacos

Ingredients

  • 8 corn tortillas warmed
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro
  • 2 tablespoons salsa
  • 1/2 lime, cut into wedges

Directions

  1. Brown ground beef in skillet over medium high heat season to taste
  2. Heat tortillas directly on a gas grill or in the microwave
  3. Place 2 tablespoons of ground beef on each taco shell
  4. Top with onions, cilantro and salsa
  5. Serve with a lime wedge
